Who we are

Fine Line, Sharper Outcomes

FINE LINE DESIGN STUDIO, LLC is a specialized design practice blending environmental graphics, wayfinding strategy, and interface design for devices and kiosks. We translate engineering, architecture, and brand intent into legible systems that work in busy, safety‑critical settings. Our studio builds from research and field testing, then delivers production‑ready artwork, fabrication specs, and ADA‑compliant messaging. We partner with facility managers, medical device teams, and marketing leaders to remove friction from complex journeys. Based in Woodland Park, Colorado, we work across the United States with a trusted network of fabricators, installers, and exhibit builders.

What we craft

Specialized Design Services

Wayfinding & Environmental Graphics

Route logic, sign schedules, and architectural integration for campuses, healthcare, and municipal sites. We map pedestrian and vehicle flows, then prototype in situ to validate legibility at distance and speed. Deliverables include message hierarchy, CAD‑based location plans, material specifications, and fabrication‑ready artwork.

Exhibit Systems & Pop‑up Retail

From 10×10 modular booths to island exhibits, we engineer message‑first environments that assemble quickly and ship light. Our kits include structural drawings, graphic skins, AV placements, and annotated crate packs. We measure success on footfall, dwell time, and post‑show pipeline, not just pretty walls.

Embedded & Kiosk UX

Interfaces for touch kiosks, medical devices, and industrial panels where seconds matter. We design for gloves, glare, and regulation—pairing icon systems with plain‑language prompts and fail‑safe states. Handoff includes UI assets, state charts, and hardware‑aware behavior specs for your firmware team.

Why teams call us

Three Reasons Our Work Lands

Field‑tested clarity

We test sightlines, contrast, and message hierarchy on location, using temporary vinyls and human‑factor checklists before final fabrication. The result is signage and screens that work for first‑time visitors, non‑native speakers, and staff under pressure.

Engineer‑level documentation

Our packets read like shop drawings: scale‑correct plans, mounting callouts, ADA references, Pantone/RAL specs, and CNC/print‑ready files. Vendors quote faster, installers improvise less, and your schedule holds.

Metrics that matter

Every brief defines success—reduced wrong‑turns, lower service calls, higher lead capture, or faster training. We wire analytics into kiosks and use observational studies at exhibits to ensure the design earns its keep.

What drives us

Mission & Solutions

Our mission

Help people keep momentum in unfamiliar spaces. Whether they are patients finding the right clinic or prospects discovering your product at a conference, our work reduces hesitation so the next step feels obvious.

Solutions that scale

We build systems, not one‑offs—sign families with extendable logic, exhibit components that re‑skin by market, and UI libraries that speed firmware releases. Your team can roll out to new sites with confidence.

Deeper dive: Embedded UX

For a diagnostic device client, we reduced mis‑taps by 43% by increasing target size, introducing progressive disclosure, and adding a guided “confirm & commit” interaction. The work shipped with bilingual strings and a zero‑downtime firmware update plan.

How we work

A Three‑Stage, No‑Drama Process

Discover & Define

We align on outcomes, audiences, constraints, and compliance. Inputs include floor plans, traffic data, device capabilities, and brand standards. You’ll receive a brief, schedule, and risk log—signed off before design begins.

Design & Prototype

We explore options, then converge quickly. Expect location plans, typographic systems, iconography, UI flows, and 3D placements as appropriate. Low‑cost on‑site mockups or clickable prototypes validate decisions with real users.

Document & Deliver

We produce fabrication drawings, print files, and developer‑ready assets with revision control. We coordinate with vendors, review proofs, and support installation or release to keep the launch on time.
